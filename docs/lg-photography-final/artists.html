How It Works

The Session Process

01

Discovery Call

We chat about your music, your aesthetic, and what you need. I'll listen to your tracks and get a feel for your vibe so we're on the same page before we shoot.

02

Planning & Moodboard

I'll put together a visual direction — locations, lighting, styling ideas. We refine it together until it feels right for your brand.

03

The Shoot

Relaxed, collaborative session where we capture a variety of looks. I'll direct but keep it natural — the best shots come when you're comfortable.

04

Delivery

Edited images delivered within 7 days via online gallery. High-res downloads plus social media crops ready to use.
