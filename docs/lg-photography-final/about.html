About Me

Nice to Meet You

I'm Luca

I'm a photographer based in Cork, Ireland, with a passion for capturing the energy of live music and events. There's something magical about being in the middle of a crowd, camera in hand, chasing the perfect moment — the split second when everything comes together.

My background is in tech (I'm also a Salesforce developer by day), but photography has always been my creative outlet. What started as shooting gigs for friends turned into a genuine obsession with visual storytelling. Now I work with indie artists, venues, festivals, and anyone who needs authentic, dynamic imagery.

I believe the best photos come from real moments — not forced poses or over-produced setups. Whether I'm shooting a headline act at a festival or press shots for an up-and-coming band, my goal is always the same: capture something genuine that connects.

My Approach

How I Work

Authentic Over Perfect

I'd rather capture a genuine moment with imperfect lighting than a stiff, perfectly-lit pose. Real emotion beats technical perfection every time.

Collaborative Process

I listen to your music, understand your brand, and work with you to create images that feel right. Every artist and every event is different.

Quality Delivery

Fast turnaround, professionally edited images, and files ready for whatever you need — web, print, or social media.
