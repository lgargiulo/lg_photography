About Me

Cork-Based Photographer with a Passion for Live Music

I'm Luca, a photographer based in Cork, Ireland, specializing in event and music photography. There's something electric about capturing live moments — the energy of a crowd, the raw emotion of a performer, the atmosphere of a venue at its peak.

I work with indie artists building their brand, venues looking to showcase their space, and anyone who needs authentic, dynamic imagery. Whether it's a festival in Dublin, a club night in Cork, or a wedding in Galway — I'm there with my camera.
