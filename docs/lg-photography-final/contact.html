FAQ

Common Questions

How far in advance should I book?

For events and weddings, I recommend reaching out at least 4-6 weeks in advance. For artist sessions, I can often accommodate shorter notice depending on availability. Last-minute bookings are sometimes possible — just ask!

Do you travel outside Cork?

Absolutely. I regularly shoot events and sessions across Ireland — Dublin, Galway, Limerick, and everywhere in between. I'm also available for European travel with advance notice. Travel costs vary depending on location.

How long until I receive my photos?

Standard turnaround is 5-7 business days for events and artist sessions. Weddings typically take 2-3 weeks. Rush delivery is available if you need images faster — just mention it in your inquiry.

What's included in your packages?

All packages include professionally edited high-resolution images, an online gallery for easy downloading and sharing, and social media crops. Full commercial usage rights are included for artist and venue work.

Do you shoot video as well?

My primary focus is photography, but I can recommend excellent videographers if you need both. For some projects, I can provide supplementary video content — let's discuss your specific needs.
